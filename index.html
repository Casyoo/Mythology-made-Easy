Welcome to Mythology made Easy - a developing quick reference guide, for things mythological and magical

About the creator...


I am a regular guy, interested in mythology, legends and the unreal or magical. Even though I believe that all myths and legends are just that, I also believe that there is nothing wrong with making the world a little more unreal and magical.


This page is a work in progress, and I make no claim that this page is 100% comprehensive, correct or complete. I'm just sharing what I find interesting. :)
